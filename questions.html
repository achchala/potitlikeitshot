Questions & FAQ

What types of pots do you offer?

We offer a variety of hand-painted ceramic pots in different sizes, colors, and designs. Each pot is unique and crafted by local artists.

How do I care for my pot?

Our pots are durable and water-resistant. For best results, wipe clean with a damp cloth and avoid harsh chemicals.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes! You can inquire about custom orders on our Contact Us page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and secure online payments.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express options are available. See our Shipping & Returns page for details.

Can I return or exchange my pot?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return window. See our Shipping & Returns page for our full policy.

What if my pot arrives damaged?

Please contact us within 48 hours with photos and we'll arrange a replacement or refund.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship to Canada, the US, and select international locations. See our Shipping & Returns page for more info.
